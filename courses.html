Courses

General English - Kids

Learn English based on your interest, language needs, or aspirations. We design lessons that suit your learning style and English language ability. This course helps you enhance your English conversation skill through intensive exercises in speaking, reading & vocabulary, listening, writing, and grammar.

General English -Adults

Whether your child has zero background in English or is aspiring to polish their English skills, we have all things covered for you. In this course, we make sure learning is fun, engaging and relevant for your child. We have extensive lessons from beginner level to advance that cater all language learning needs of our learners.

Business English

This course offers our learners intensive business English lessons. From learning useful business English expressions to making a presentation in English, writing emails, facilitating a meeting, telephoning, teleconference…and more. Not only that we help you learn based on your business English needs, we offer you the flexibility of your time too. In this course, when at times you can’t attend classes, we still assist you with your language needs at all times possible. Whether it’s about submitting your homework, seeking language assistance, helping you with your reports or presentation script, and so on… we are here for you!

    If the student cannot attend the class due to some circumstances, we offer make up class, it means the schedule can be moved to another time or day but depending on the availability of the teacher.

    Uninformed absences are considered used session. So non-refundable or no make up class.
