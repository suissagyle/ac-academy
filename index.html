About Us

Easy English was founded in 2006. We have been mainly teaching Japanese learners from different ages and English language level. We cater every language learners’ specific needs and goals. As a matter of fact, we customize lessons for each learner.

Our utmost goal is to be able to deliver cutting edge learning experiences to our learners. We aspire excellence and quality at all times, and giving meaningful learning to our learners is our ultimate goal.

As we all know, there are many ways to learn a language nowadays. However, we strongly believe that learning a language, specifically, from a great teacher is certainly one of the most effective ways to master a language effectively. We don’t only provide our learners effective learning. We also put high value in our learners’ time, money, and effort. Hence, giving our learners the best and most meaningful learning experience is our biggest motivation.
